// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm a Year 10 student at Hume Anglican Grammar on an Academic Scholarship, passionate about engineering and the way things work at a fundamental level.

I've been building and tinkering with electronics since early high school — soldering circuits, designing 3D models in Tinkercad, and writing code in Python and HTML. I don't just want to understand how technology works; I want to build it.

Outside of school, I'm working through a full electronics and Arduino project roadmap — moving from sensor modules to fully soldered, from-scratch circuit builds. Every project goes on this portfolio.

I'm an elected Class Senator (2017, 2020, 2022) and consistent academic award recipient — I bring the same discipline to engineering that I bring to the classroom.
